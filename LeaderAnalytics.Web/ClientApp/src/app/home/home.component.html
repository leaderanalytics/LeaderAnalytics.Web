Who we are

Leader Analytics is a software development company located in Oceanside, CA. Our founder began writing business software in the late 1980's to solve tough problems in the manufacturing industry. He than moved on to implement systems in finance, banking, and logistics. Over a period of three decades he accumulated a portfolio of software development techniques and best practices that form the foundation of what is now Leader Analytics.

Today our company continues to focus on refining our development processes, acquiring the best talent in the business, and selecting tools and technologies that enable us to create world class software. From a development perspective, our core competencies are Microsoft .Net and the entire suite of Microsoft technologies and platforms. We write business applications for companies of all sizes across a variety of industries. Our mission is to efficiently create software that meets or exceeds our customers expectations.
