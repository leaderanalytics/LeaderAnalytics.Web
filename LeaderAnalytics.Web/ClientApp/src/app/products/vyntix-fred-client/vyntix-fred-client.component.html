Vyntix FRED Client

Utility for downloading economic data from St. Louis Federal Reserve (FRED)

Easily Use FRED data from within your application. FRED client acquires data from the FRED API and returns it to you as strongly typed dotnet objects. FRED client populates vintage dates across all observations - even when downloading observations in chunks.
